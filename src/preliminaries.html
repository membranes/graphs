Problem Statement
At present, research desks manually determine whether a dispatch is associated with a set of categories of interest.  The categories include geopolitical terms, geographies, organisations, times, etc.  These are coarse level categories; a subsequent phase will focus on fine level categories, e.g., organisation type.  The categories of interest include categories that are not included in any text detection & classification software; open source & commercial software.  Hence, our decision to develop a custom model.


				

					
Outcome Expectations / Underlying Aims
 In brief:
					
    
						
  • A reduction in detection time per A4 page; from approximately 2.5 minutes to < 9 seconds. (A4, ~250 words,
							~25 lines, 10 pt font, ~1.25-inch line spacing)
						
    • 
						
  • Minimal mis-classifications per category; the false negative rate of each category must not exceed the category's limit.
